What Is IAS chapter

The Industry Applications Society, as a transnational organization, is interested in advancement of the theory and practice of electrical and electronic engineering in the development, design, manufacture and application of electrical systems, apparatus, devices and controls to the processes and equipment of industry

Mission

IEEE Industry Applications Society enables the advancement of theory and practice in the design, development, manufacturing and application of safe, sustainable, reliable, smart electrical systems, equipment and services.

Vision

IEEE Industry Applications Society will be a world leader in the advancement of science and technology linking theory and practice in the application of electrical and electronic systems for the benefit of humanity.

About IEEE SB RIT

One of the most vibrant and largest student branches in the Kerala section, IEEE SB RIT had made a mark for itself over the years. From being a part of various inter SB IEEE programs to hosting our own events, it have come a long way. In 2013, IEEE RIT SB was adjudged as the BEST STUDENT BRANCH. It is one of the oldest and most active student branches in Kerala section, with around 150 members joining every year. IEEE RIT SB has always believed in providing its student members with opportunities to develop and showcase their skills and research at higher platforms. The enthusiasm with which its members participate in various programs is awe-inspiring. This SB is sure to scale great heights in the years to come.
